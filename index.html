How to Set Up and Activate Trend Micro Internet or Maximum Security with Your Activation Code

What Trend Micro Does

Trend Micro helps protect your computer and personal information from viruses, hackers, and other online threats. Once it's installed and activated, it keeps you safe automatically with regular updates and real-time protection.

Which Product Do You Have?

Before starting, check which version you purchased:

  • Trend Micro Internet Security – For Windows computers; offers strong virus protection and privacy tools.
  • Trend Micro Maximum Security – Works on Windows, Mac, Android, and iOS; includes password manager and identity protection.

Where to Find Your Activation Code

Your activation code is a 20-letter and number key used to start your subscription. You can find it in one of these places:

  • Boxed Product: Printed on the card inside the box.
  • Online Purchase: Sent to your email after you buy.
  • Trend Micro Account: Log in to your Trend Micro account to view your code.

Step 1: Download the Installer

Here's how to download the installer:

  1. Open your internet browser and go to https://www.trendmicro.com/activate .
  2. Sign in with your Trend Micro account.
  3. Type in your code.
  4. Choose your product (Internet Security or Maximum Security).
  5. Click Download to save the installer file to your computer.

Step 2: Install Trend Micro

Once the file is downloaded:

  1. Open your folder and double-click the installer.
  2. If your computer asks for permission, click Yes or Allow.
  3. Read and accept the License Agreement.
  4. Follow the on-screen steps until the setup is complete.

Step 3: Activate Your Trend Micro

When the installation is done:

  1. Enter your 20-character activation code when asked.
  2. Sign in to your Trend Micro account (or create one if you don't have it).
  3. Check your subscription details.
  4. Click Activate to finish the setup.

If You Have Problems Installing or Activating

Try these simple fixes:

  • Check your computer: Make sure it meets the minimum system requirements.
  • Recheck your code: Look for typing mistakes like confusing "O" and "0."
  • Check your internet: You need a stable connection for downloading and activation.
  • Remove other antivirus programs: They might interfere with Trend Micro.

Moving Trend Micro to a New Device

If you want to use Trend Micro on a different computer or phone:

  1. Uninstall it from your old device.
  2. Go back to https://www.trendmicro.com/activate .
  3. Download the installer again.
  4. Install and activate it using your existing activation code.

Manage Your Trend Micro Account
